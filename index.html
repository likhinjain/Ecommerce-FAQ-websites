Order Management

My order has already shipped. Can I still cancel it?
Unfortunately, shipped orders cannot be cancelled. You can initiate a return instead.
Can I cancel my order?
Yes, if it hasn’t been delivered yet. Go to the 'Main Menu,' select 'Cancel,' choose the item, provide a reason, and follow the instructions.
How do I modify my order?
Contact support immediately. Changes are only possible before shipping.
What happens if I ordered the wrong item?
You can cancel or return it once delivered. Contact support for help.
Can I add items to an existing order?
No, but you can place a new order. Bundled checkout options are coming soon.
Will I be charged for cancellations?
No cancellation fee if done before shipping. After shipping, return policies apply.

Order Status & Tracking

How long does it take to process an order?
Most orders are processed within 24–48 hours. Delays may occur during peak seasons.
Why was my order cancelled?
Possible reasons include payment failure, stock unavailability, or address issues. You’ll receive a notification with details.
My order shows delivered but I haven’t received it. What should I do?
Please connect with a live agent for assistance.
Why hasn’t my tracking number updated yet?
Tracking updates may take up to 24 hours after dispatch.
Can I change the delivery address after placing an order?
Address changes are only possible before the order is shipped. Connect with live agents for help.
